How to Join

The ZAO is a gated community. There's a path in - and a clear place to start.

Start here

New and want to add your time and energy? Do these in order. This is the front door.

  1. Listen. Hear the music at thezao.com. Music first - that's the whole point.
  2. Show up. Join Farcaster, follow @zaal, and drop into a Monday fractal (6pm EST).
  3. Pick a door. Artist, builder, or supporter - see below.
  4. Say hi. DM @zaal on Farcaster. Tell us what you want to work on. We point you at the right group.

Not sure how the pieces fit together? Read the org chart first.

The three doors

Pick the one that fits. Each is a different kind of commitment - we keep it honest.

  1. Artists. Release through The ZAO, show up in fractals, collaborate. The community exists for you first. This is the deepest commitment and the most support.
  2. Builders. Ship something that helps - a tool, a fix, an event. Earn Respect for the work you do. Builder engagement runs through ZABAL Games (the internal toolstack identity), but you are joining The ZAO.
  3. Listeners and supporters. Back the community as a fan. You can hold $ZABAL, the community token on Base - it is one way to support and carries governance weight. It is a token inside The ZAO, not the umbrella. No financial advice; hold only what you choose to.
