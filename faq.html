What's ZABAL?

ZABAL is The ZAO's internal builder identity - the toolstack the team builds with, and the home of ZABAL Games (the outsider-facing builder engagement program). It is not the umbrella brand. The ZAO is the umbrella; ZABAL is how we build it. There is also $ZABAL, the community token on Base - it carries incentives and governance weight inside The ZAO. The token is a piece inside the network, not the network itself. No financial advice. See the org chart for where ZABAL sits.
