03 — Operating System

The tools that make the org run. Chat. Music player. Events. Payments. Agents. Every tool is owned by the community, forkable, and interoperable.

  • ZAO OS - the community chat client and home base.
  • ZOE - the agent that helps run the org day to day.
  • ZABAL / ZABAL Games - the internal builder identity and toolstack. ZABAL is how The ZAO gets built; ZABAL Games is the outsider-facing program where new builders come in. It powers the work - it is not the top-level brand. (See the org chart.)
  • WaveWarZ - an incubated project, on its way to its own sub-DAO.

Every tool is owned by the community, forkable, and interoperable.
