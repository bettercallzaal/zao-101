How all of it fits together, in one picture. The one-line model: The ZAO is the umbrella - an incubator and impact network. Everything else is either a project incubated under it, a partnership beside it, or the internal toolstack that builds it.

Naming rules (so The ZAO stops getting eclipsed)

  1. Say "The ZAO" first. In any external comm - to partners, on decks, on pamphlets, in group names - The ZAO is the lead brand. ZABAL is not the headline.
  2. ZABAL is the internal builder identity. Use it for the toolstack and the Games. It does not stand in for The ZAO as the umbrella.
  3. $ZABAL the token is fine as-is. It is a deployed on-chain community token on Base. A piece inside the network, not the umbrella.
  4. Partners get "community partnership," not "project under The ZAO." COC Concertz and similar are partnerships with equal billing. Respect their ownership.
  5. Incubated vs partnership is the key distinction. Incubated = ZAO-owned with cofounders (WaveWarZ, ZAO Festivals). Partnership = beside The ZAO (COC). Do not blur them.
